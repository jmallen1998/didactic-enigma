About Us

Trusted Link Technical Solutions is a veteran-owned IT company founded by husband-and-wife team Joe and Jessie Allen, proudly based in Niceville, FL. We're a team of two with a mission to bring trustworthy, reliable, and professional IT solutions to small businesses across the Emerald Coast.

Who We Are

Joe Allen
Founder & Cybersecurity Lead

Jessie Allen
Co-Founder & Technical Strategist

Joe is a cybersecurity engineer and Service-Disabled Veteran with over 9 years of experience across military and private-sector environments. He specializes in securing networks, protecting data, and giving small organizations the confidence of enterprise-grade tech.

Jessie brings a background in web development, technical support, and creative problem-solving. She’s driven by details and dedicated to making technology more human, helpful, and approachable.

Together, we started Trusted Link to help local businesses stop struggling with tech and start thriving with it.

Why We Do It

Big corporations have entire IT teams — small businesses don’t. That’s where we come in. We believe every business deserves dependable tech, no matter its size or budget.

Our faith shapes our values: trust, reliability, and a genuine desire to help others. We treat every client like a partner — and every challenge like it's our own.
